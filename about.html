About

Well, what is HazTek?

You may know HazTek as the company that powers the US, but there is much more to us! HazTek is a research facility that specializes in energy production, biotech research, and weaponry production. HazTek is most known for its high-tech weaponry, its massive advancements in genetic engineering, and the Core Stabilizer, a feat of engineering never seen before!

HazTek is most known for covering 95% of America's power, resulting in 250% lower power bills! We are also known for founding and maintaining Dirt County, as well as the neighboring Maple County.

HazTek also provides the US military with over 75% of their tech and weaponry. Also, the HazTek Military Operations Force provides safety to many large US cities, and even countries outside of the US!
