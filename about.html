Well, what is HazTek?

You may know HazTek as the company that powers the US, but there is much more to us! HazTek is a research facility that specializes in energy production, biotech research, and weaponry production. HazTek is most known for its high-tech weaponry, its massive advancements in genetic engineering, and the Core Stabilizer, a feat of engineering never seen before!
